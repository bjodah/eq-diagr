Concentration units

  • molal is a unit for concentration = moles / (kg of water)

    Molality is therefore a concentration unit that is temperature- and pressure-independent.

  • molar = moles / (1L of aqueous solution), is a concentration unit that is T- and p-dependent.

At 25°C the density of dilute aqueous solutions is ≈1000  g/L, and molality ≈ molarity; however the density varies with concentration, temperature, etc.
