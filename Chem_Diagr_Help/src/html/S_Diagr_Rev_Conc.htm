SPANA / Making a diagram

Example of a diagram where reversed concentration ranges are used. The diagram shows the species distribution for Fe3+ when chloride is exchanged for sulphate. Note that both Cl− and SO42− are varied along the X-axis, although only one component is shown in the X-axis of the diagram. The composition changes from 0.4 M HCl at the left of the diagram to 0.2 M H2SO4 at the right (see last two lines of the input file below). The ionic strength is evaluated iteratively at each point.
