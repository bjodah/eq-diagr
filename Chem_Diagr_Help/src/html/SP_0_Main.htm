Chemical Equilibrium Diagrams
  
  

SED and PREDOM
  SED & PREDOM are two programs which calculate and draw chemical equilibrium diagrams. They are the number crunching machinery behind the program SPANA.
  • SED: creates fraction, logarithmic and solubility diagrams
  • PREDOM: creates predominance area diagrams
  • Both programs use the HaltaFall algorithm.
  • There are programs to convert plot files: to PostScript or to PDF
